    There are 8 different bears- brown bear,black bear,spirit bear, Kodiak bear, grizzly bear, panda bear,spectacled bear;

    Adult bears can run up to an incredible 40mph, which is the same pace as a greyhound and breaking the speed limit on many roads in the UK.

    Bears come in many different colors, shape, and sizes.

Bart the Bear (January 19, 1977 – May 10, 2000) was a male Alaskan Kodiak bear best known for his numerous appearances in Hollywood films, including The Bear (for which he received widespread acclaim), White Fang, Legends of the Fall, and The Edge.

Adult bears are solitary and usually avoid contact with humans; however, they may attack if starving or to protect cubs. Bears are hunted for their skins and flesh. The flesh of polar bears in particular often contains trichina worms, which can be transmitted to humans if the meat is not thoroughly cooked . Polar bears and grizzly bears have been affected by the loss of habitat due to global warming and human encroachment on their territory.
